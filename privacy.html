Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

Pact ("we", "the app") is a screen time management app that helps individuals block distracting apps on their own iOS devices. Your privacy is important to us.

Data We Collect

  • Email address — for account creation and authentication via Firebase Auth.
  • Display name — derived from your email, shown to your accountability partner.
  • Partner pairing — when you pair with a friend, we store the connection in our database.
  • Unlock requests — we store request status (pending/approved/denied) to facilitate the accountability flow.
  • Streak data — consecutive days of discipline, stored to show your progress.
  • Push notification token — to send you notifications when your partner sends a request.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We never see which apps you block. Apple's Screen Time API uses opaque tokens — we cannot access app names or bundle identifiers.
  • We do not collect usage duration, screen recordings, screenshots, or browsing history.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the app's core functionality: account management, partner pairing, unlock request flow, and push notifications.

Data Storage

Data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), hosted in the United States. Blocked app selections are stored locally on your device only.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at yesaket@icloud.com.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication (Google)
  • Cloud Firestore (Google)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google)
